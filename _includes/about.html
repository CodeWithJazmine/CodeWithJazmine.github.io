About

Jazmine Chargualaf is a Game Developer specializing in Technical Art, blending game development with shaders, lighting, and VFX.

With experience in Unreal Engine 5 and Unity, she is continuously expanding her skills in material graphs and visual effects to enhance game worlds with immersive atmospheres. Jazmine is currently working on Moonlit Alchemy, a 2.5D game featuring enchanting potion crafting and dynamic lighting, and previously developed Neon Exodus, a solo stealth game created in Unity.

She holds a degree in Game Development and has a deep love for storytelling, cozy aesthetics, and worldbuilding. Whether experimenting with shaders or refining game mechanics, Jazmine is passionate about creating visually stunning and immersive experiences.

Her goal is to work in AAA or indie as a Game Developer or Technical Artist, while continuing to grow as a solo developer. When she’s not crafting games, she’s likely lost in a book, exploring new ideas, or making the perfect cup of coffee.
